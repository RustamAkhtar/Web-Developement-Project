About Bedwars

A little bit about Roblox Bedwars

Roblox Bedwars is a an intriguing game on Roblox that was originally copied form MineCraft an changed slightly. However the gameplay is different and more exciting. There are engaging levels the keep increasing the more you play and the more xp you gain. Some gamemodes in Bedwars are squads, solos and doubles.


Bedwars Gamemodes

Squads

A gamemode in Bedwars is squads. In this gamemode there are four teams of four who have to each try to win the game.

Solos
