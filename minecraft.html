About Minecraft

A little bit about Mineraft

Minecraft is a an intriguing game with two main games survival and creative. in survival you must survive creepers that are monsters who try to kill you during the night. In creative you can build anything you want with any materials you want.


Minecraft Gamemodes

Survival

One of the gamemodes in Minecraft is survival in which you have to survive for as long as you can by building a house and defeating creepers.

Creative

Another gamemode in Minecraft is creative in which you can do whatever you want with infinite materials to build.

Adventure

This gamemode is adventure gamemode in which there are custom maps and there is a story you have to follow.
