A little bit about Rocket League

Rocket League is a an intriguing game with cars that play football. There are two teams of cars that have to try to kick the huge ball into the opposing team's goal. After five minutes the team with the most goals wins. Some gamemodes in Rocket League are squads trio dous.


Rocket League Gamemodes

A gamemode in Rocket League is squads in which two teams of four have to score as many goals as possible.
